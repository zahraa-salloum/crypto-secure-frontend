🔒 Client-Side Encryption

✓ Your encryption key NEVER leaves your browser!
All encryption and decryption happens locally on your device. The key is never sent to the server, ensuring maximum privacy.

🔐 RC4 Stream Cipher

  • Developed by Ron Rivest in 1987
  • Variable key size (40-2048 bits)
  • Used in SSL/TLS and WEP
  • Known vulnerabilities: Biased keystream, related-key attacks

📡 A5/1 Stream Cipher

  • Used in GSM mobile encryption
  • 64-bit key, LFSR-based design
  • Three shift registers (19, 22, 23 bits)
  • Known vulnerabilities: Can be broken with moderate resources
